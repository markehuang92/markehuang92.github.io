"Grumps"

1881 - 1979

Grumps aka Ngin Ngin was Voy's mother. Linda kept Grumps busy by asking her to re-hem all her skirts and dresses to "mini" level as was the trend at the time in the 70's. Grumps also passed the time by cutting out beautiful stencils. Read Linda's story about Grumps in the photo gallery.
