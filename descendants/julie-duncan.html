Julie Duncan

My sister Julie and I are only 17 months apart in age. Growing up, people always thought we were twins, which we never liked. We've grown to embrace our special closeness and have on occasion coincidentally dressed alike, or had similar thoughts.

When we were thinking of a name for our family partnership, we both came up independently with "Five Rivers", symbolizing the 5 siblings from Riverside.

(Linda- Jan 18, 2016)


Five Rivers

One after another streams flowed north

Three by the lair of a bear

One not far from the golden gate

and the last one into a valley farthest north

Time passed

Streams became rivers to bring forth more streams

Remembering their source, a confluence formed

To renew the strength of their connection and heritage

(By Julie for her holiday card- 2010.)



Parent of Emily Wong Duncan and Eric Wong Duncan.
